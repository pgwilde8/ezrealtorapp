Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I hit my lead limit?

You'll get alerts at 80% and 95%. If you hit the limit, we'll automatically bump you to the next tier for the remainder of the month and pro-rate the difference. Maximum 1 tier increase per month.

Can I prevent auto-bumping?

Yes! Toggle "Pause at cap" in your dashboard settings. We'll pause lead processing instead of upgrading automatically. You can then manually upgrade or wait until next month.

What's the forgiveness system?

Once per quarter, you can drop back to your previous tier if an auto-bump was due to a temporary spike. Perfect for seasonal fluctuations.

Are the bundles guaranteed?

Bundles are processing limits, not guarantees of visitors or leads. All resources (emails, SMS, voice, AI tokens) are pooled across your pages and reset monthly.

Can I downgrade my plan?

Yes! If your usage stays below 60% of your current tier for 2 consecutive months, we'll suggest a downgrade option for your next billing cycle.
